Rhys’s Music Reviews

Whole Lotta Red has some very interesting production, the beats on this album were pretty good, but it was neither the production nor the beats that left me unsatisfied. It was the bloated tracklist and the very repetitive lyrics, I understand the whole concert songs where they are meant to be chanted at a concert on when you're having a good time with your friends. But I don't think it works that well for music you'd want to listen to any time alone or with 1 or 2 friends. As well as the vampire/punk whole ascetic is very unappealing to me although I could see how some people like it. The use of vocals is very interesting as after listening to this album a few times you start to focus less on the annoying moaning and more on the words that Carti is "Singing". Some tracks on here were a little too repetitive for me like jump out the house and meh whether it was the beat or the lyrics. I feel like a lot of the longer songs on this album could have used a little beat switch, the melodies were very repetitive if they did not get switched up at all. I way I think carti could have made this album a lot better along with shortening it would have been having more features, I do usually enjoy carti's when he has got a verse or 2 on a track along with another 1 or 2 artists. Although I'm glad that he did not make a die-lit 2, The album would be a whole lot better if it was shorter. I much rather revisit his other album die lit over whole lotta red, a maybe listen 6.5/10
