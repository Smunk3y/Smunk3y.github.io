Rhys’s Music Reviews

Yeezus, what an odd album it's got it's up and it down’s for sure, where the track’s like New Slaves and Hold my liquor, are amazing and the tracks like I Am A God, Guilt Trip, I'm In It, and Send It up are all on the lower end spectrum of this album, the album starts the tone of perfectly with On Sight blasting synths in your ears and then the amazing sample, I've read online that this album is meant to represent a character named yeezus that gets overloaded with fame, but he’s depressed so he turns to drugs and partying and he later on in the albums finds god and love and stops all of the bad he was doing, I've never seen it though. The song New Slaves has some controversial verses but the ending with Frank Ocean is probably one of the best if not the best song outro Kanye has ever done. With hold my liquor is one of my favorite Kanye verses. Although I'm in it may be one of the worst songs ever. It's got some really bad lyrics but the production is good. And I am a god's beat sound like it's just an air raid siren being rapped over Yeezus is a very up-and-down album that is a 6.5 / 10, I wish just 3 more songs got shaved off by Rick Rubin.
