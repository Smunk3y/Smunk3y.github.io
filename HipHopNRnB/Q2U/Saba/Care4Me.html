Rhys’s Music Reviews

CARE FOR ME by saba, what an album from yet another great Chicago artist, the delivery by saba on this album is incredible, he gets his story and or message to you very well. The songs busy/sirens and prom/king are the most vivid story's in my opinion. Both those songs have a great beat switch as well, the beats on this album are very chilled and laid back. The chill delivery coming from saba also is crazy, then when he turns right around and switches up his flow and or beat but keeps the story straight is incredible. This album is a well put together album and saba gets his message across very well in a well put together album.
