Endless by Frank Ocean is one of the best-sequenced albums ever made, is very experimental, and had got quite the backstory, it was live-streamed as Frank building a " Staircase to heaven " on apple music for 140 hours. During this Livestream, random instrumental tracks from the album would play. The whole album was made just as a ploy to Def jam records, the ploy profited Frank ocean 28M Usd, and got him out of his shitty record deal, now to the music part of the album. It has got the kind of vibe where every song is all morphed into 1. The starting song at your best sets the tone well, it's going to be an emotional album and very raw, although Frank Ocean may have not put as much emotion into endless as say Blond or even Channel Orange, due to the raw fell of endless it comes across really well if not better than his other projects. The album if thought of as a rollercoaster, an emotional rollercoaster would start at about 30M, drop down head back up to 50M at wither, drop a little bit, and then steadily incline to Rushes at 125M then have a very steep drop until the album finishes off. Most of the songs on this album feel short and unfished, now the short does not matter that much, but the unfinished feel on this album was intentional to have a rawer feel, this album is somthing very unike and special, and has a very deep emotional depth. it's hard to explain this album so just go listen to it, either on apple music or download it from the link below the album cover. Although experimental its 100% a recommendation, 9/10
