Rhys’s Music Reviews

Channel orange, is an amazing album by Frank Ocean, not the common favorite cause it gets overshadowed by blond way too much as it's still an amazing album. The storytelling on these songs is crazy good and some have very deep meanings, you could look at this album in two different ways one way some good-sounding chill music or very deep and rich in storytelling. You'll only find the deep storytelling and meanings if you sit down do nothing and read the lyrics, or search online for them. One of my Favorite Frank ocean songs "Pyramids" has got a very deep meaning behind it other than the fact that it sounds good. The first part is about Cleopatra the great Egyptian ruler and the 2nd half is about a modern-day woman whos a sex worker at the las vegas Pyramid Hotel. A load of songs have super complex meanings and shine a light on some sort of racism or problem in America. Bad Religion for example show's how the term " Allah Hu Akbar " has been dragged through the dirt by the American culture when the exact translation just is " God is Great " , amazing production and the fact that this album was made in 3 weeks is crazy with all the meanings and relations to real life. There is a theory online about super-rich kids being about a rich kid who's addicted to drugs and has all the money in the world not knowing the consequences of what could happen and the fact that frankly says " there's nothing like this kind of view " would be in relation to an amazing high that this drug-addicted teen can't get enough of. As well as the line " You'll say I jump I never do " is in relation to the people close to him letting him know he's going to overdose but he thinks he's invincible. Overall this album has amazing production and out-of-the-world storytelling. would 100% recommend a listen 9 / 10.
