I've started a journey on building a nixie tube clock there are a lot of things you need to build one, you need a whole list of parts which is on the right. High knowledge of electrical components. A good steady hand to solder with, and 200-300$. But they are super cool and cozy-looking so it's 100% Worth it. They remind me of a cozy fire near Christmas time with that nice orange glow. I oreded mine from ukraine, there are datasheets and websites I've found on them they will all be linked below, datasheets, build guides, etc!
