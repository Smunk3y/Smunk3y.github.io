About The Site

I Enjoy listening to music and enjoy coding so I thought I'd make a website to share my opinions on music and try and improve my writing skills with written reviews. Anything music-related may pop up in this section of the website, although any physical things such as listing equipment will be listed in the vinyl section of the site. All of the opinions are mine and the written reviews are written by me, if you disagree with the reviews then that's tough cause it's my opinion, not yours, you can always challenge it though. The Goal is about 1 Written review a week, and if I find extra time ill work on something like a top 10 list or a extra review.

How To Use The Site

The nav bar at the very top is to switch between the main four sections of the site. Here in the music section artists are divided into two categories, then into alphabet groups. Click on either HIP-HOP or ROCK/ALT for the alphabet catagoires to pop down, then click the artists your looking for's respective alphabet group. The alphabet groups are sorted by first and second letter only. Find your artist and click on him to find the rating's of their albums ive listend to. If your only looking for written reviews click the REVIEW on the navbar to list written reviews.

Written Reviews

Most albums will have just a number below them, but the ones in the review tab have a written review & tracklist rating to go along with it. You can check the review tab to search for a album with a written review, or when checking out a artist you can hover over the album, if it makes a underline it's got a written review. Click on one's with underlines to see the reivew.
