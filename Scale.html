What the Rating's Mean (Relatively)
10 • Peak of music!
9 • Amazing Album, Few bad parts or not much influence
8 • A few skips, Rest are strong tracks
7 • A few skips, Rest are good tracks
6 • Pretty good, Would listen again
5 • Average, Not good not bad
4 • Not amazing, May listen again
3 • Below avrage, Would rather not listen again
0-2 • Not good, Would not listen again, Unless paid

Album ratings don't have to follow this exact structure, they can be upped or down by a few things. Some are the layout of the album, if the album has a theme or not, If the album feels like a compilation of songs or something just to get hit songs. If there is a story behind the album or if there is a reason the band made it, the imapct it had on music, those are the main reasons but each album rating can be influenced by other reasons, Just remember that music is subjective and if you don't like my opinion please challenge me in the form below, I may change my mind.
