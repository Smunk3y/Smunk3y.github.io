How To Navagate

Up near the top their is a few sections of text with a " " , if you click on them they will drop down a menu with the letters A-F, G-K, Etc. Click on one of these to get the artist with their name starting with a letter in that category and genre, after that click on the artist you want to see the albums I've reviewed , and click on the album to get a tracklist review and a piece of text about the album. The extra tab works the same way but with non-artist/album reviews, things like suggesting an album or my vinyl collection! If you're interested in the upcoming albums those will be under the tab " Extra " and if the one you wanted's not there suggest it.

Suggest A Album

Under the extra's tab, there is a part that says " Suggest A Album" if you click on it, and fill out the google form, I should review that album in the next week or so unless the album doesn't exist.
