Rhys’s Music Reviews

My Beautiful dark twisted fantasy by Kanye West is one of my favorite albums of all time, the use of real instruments and instantly complex production with some very good bars makes this album near perfect. The opening track dark fantasy sets the tone well, but the starting vocal performance seems a little off, but that then moves into one of the most underrated songs of all time has 3 amazing Kanye bars and a really fun beat, after this, we have the song power which I like and its very complex but it's not quite as strong as some of the other songs like Devil In A New Dress, Lost In The World or Runaway, where either they have an amazing sample or a genius song structure, in the situation of Devil In A New Dress the Kanye verse at the start and that little pause it makes for the nice guitar rift before rick ross comes in I love, the sample on Devil In A New dress is also super well done, this project also has one of my favorite interludes ever All Of The Lights interlude, the violin and the piano which is played by Elthon Jhon is super well put together for a nice 1-minute break from any vocals. The song Hell Of A Life is good but compared to the other songs on the album does not quite reach the level of music, I would 100% recommend you listen to this album in a good mood without anything else to focus on there are so many small details you won't pick up if your cleaning while listening, 10 / 10 Album, Top 5, Hip Hop
