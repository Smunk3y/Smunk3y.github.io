This album was amazing from the humming by kudi to the production by kanye, the songs that got 9/10 i felt were a little unbalanced for example on kids see ghosts the song the start is a little annoying but kanye's verse makes up for the whole songs flaws, the nice guitar riff on feel the love near the end just felt as though it calmed that song down really well although i don't believe that song was the best fit for the album. The strong songs that were my favorite were Cudi Montage and 4th Dimension, on Cudi montage the acoustic guitar sounds amazing paired with Mr. Hudson's voice there at the end. Then on 4th dimension where kanye sampled a 80 year old christmas song has raps his ass off on it was amazing, the album was short but that’s a good thing perfect length amazing album 10/10
